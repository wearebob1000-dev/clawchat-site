PumpPal Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Introduction

PumpPal ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered fitness companion developed by WEARE Inc. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application and services.

Information We Collect

When you use PumpPal, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Health & Fitness Data: Data from connected wearable devices (such as WHOOP), including heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), sleep metrics, recovery scores, strain data, and workout information.
  • Account Information: Email address and profile information used to create and manage your account.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and preferences set.
  • Communication Data: Messages and inputs you provide when interacting with the AI fitness companion.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized fitness and health recommendations
  • Analyze your training load and recovery to prevent overtraining
  • Generate insights about your sleep quality and patterns
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI coaching
  • Communicate with you about your fitness progress

Third-Party Services

PumpPal integrates with third-party health platforms (such as WHOOP) to access your fitness data. When you connect a third-party service, we access only the data you authorize. We do not sell your health data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your health and fitness data is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. We prioritize local-first data storage when possible to minimize exposure.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disconnect third-party services at any time

Children's Privacy

PumpPal is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or by email.
